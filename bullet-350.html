The Royal Enfield Bullet 350, is one of the most popular retro-roadster bikes in India. It is one of the most iconic motorcycles ever sold by Royal Enfield and holds the unofficial title of the world’s longest-running production bike, as it has been produced continuously since 1931. The Bullet 350 continues with the authentic retro design with chrome and gold highlights, a halogen headlight, an analogue speedometer, and a single-piece seat. It gets the same 350cc engine from the Royal Enfield Classic 350.
