The Royal Enfield Himalayan 450 is an adventure bike from the Chennai-based manufacturer. The ADV is a successor to the now-discontinued Himayalan 411 and features the Royal Enfield’s first-ever liquid-cooled, single-cylinder engine. It can easily handle everyday commutes, long-distance touring, and hardcore off-riding.
